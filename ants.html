question for the ages: once ants gain sentience and unite across colonies, will they take over the world??



according to biomass things, the total mass of species in an area is about the same as each other, so total mass of humans on earth should be about equal to total mass of ants on earth:
7,077,551,385 humans on earth * 150 lbs avg weight of a human = 1,061,632,707,750 avg lbs of humans on earth (~ 1.1 * 10 ^ 12 lbs)

1.5 mg ~ 0.0000033069 lbs ~ 3.3 * 10^ -6 lbs avg weight of an ant

1,061,632,707,750 / 0.0000033069 = total human lbs / single ant weight = 321,035,624,829,901,000 ants alive today

that’s 45359702.4 ants per person, assuming one ant can lift 50 times their body weight (conservative estimate),

45359702.4 ants can lift 45359702.4 * 0.0000033069 * 50 = number ants * weight * 50 times = 7499.99999 ~ 7500 lbs

which is much greater than the average weight of 150 lbs per person.

in conclusion, yes, once ants realise their power and join forces, they will take over our world.
